Pet Health Care

At Pawsome Friends Adoption Centre, we take the health and well-being of our furry friends very seriously. Our team of experienced veterinarians and animal care professionals work together to provide top-notch medical care to our animals.

Vaccinations and Preventative Care

We ensure that all our animals are up-to-date on their vaccinations and receive regular check-ups to prevent illnesses.

Medical Treatment and Surgery

In the event of an illness or injury, our on-site medical facility is equipped to provide necessary treatment and surgery to get our animals back to full health.

Rehabilitation and Therapy

We also offer rehabilitation and therapy services to help our animals recover from injuries or surgeries, and to improve their overall health and well-being.

Adoption Medical Guarantee

We stand behind the health of our animals and offer a medical guarantee to our adopters. If any health issues arise within a certain timeframe after adoption, we will work with you to provide necessary medical care and support.
